CYBER PUZZLE

Click shapes to pick them up, move with your mouse, and click the board to place. When no more pieces can fit, you lose!

HOW TO PLAY

🎮 GAMEPLAY MECHANICS
  • Click a shape in the dock to pick it up — it follows your mouse.
  • Click the board to place the shape (no dragging required).
  • Fill any **horizontal row** or **vertical column** to clear it!
  • **Rotation:** Click 🔄 on a dock block, or press **R** / **Space** / **↑** while carrying a piece.
  • **Next Wave:** Preview the next 3 shapes below the dock before you use up the current batch.
  • **Streak Bonus:** Clear lines on consecutive turns to multiply combo points (up to ×2).
⚠️ GAME OVER STATE

When the grid becomes too full and **none** of your available shapes in the dock can fit anywhere on the remaining empty spaces, the game ends.

🕹️ MODES

Arcade Mode: Pure tactical challenge. Score high before locking up.

Zen Mode: Relaxed play. Enjoy **UNDO** actions. If you block yourself, a Zen Healing clears a portion of the board to let you keep going!

2 Player Battle: Side-by-side boards. Each player picks from their own dock. When you can't place any pieces, you're locked — the other keeps playing. Highest score wins!
